Solo payout address
This is the address ckpool uses when generating blocks. Restart the app after saving.
Use a Fractal Bitcoin payout address. Supported formats include bc1q..., bc1p..., 1..., and 3.... The address will be validated by the connected Fractal node before saving.
Difficulty (vardiff)
ckpool adjusts share difficulty per miner to keep share rates reasonable. Pick a preset based on your largest miner (Fast share rates can overwhelm the pool if difficulty is too low).
Presets are per miner, not your total fleet hashrate. Use the band that each device sits in. Setting difficulty too high will not help find blocks. It just reduces how often shares are submitted and can waste hashing effort.
Whole-number difficulties only (ckpool). Changing difficulty requires an app restart.
